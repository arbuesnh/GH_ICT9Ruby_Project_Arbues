5 Ways to Prevent Fires in Your Home

Fires can cause devastating damage to homes and put lives at risk. Fortunately, there are many ways to prevent fires from happening in the first place. Here are six tips for preventing fires in your home:

1. Install smoke alarms in every room
Make sure that there is a smoke alarm in every room of your home, especially in the kitchen and near bedrooms. Smoke alarms can detect smoke and alert you to a fire, giving you time to evacuate.
2. Keep flammable materials away from heat sources
Flammable materials like curtains, paper, and cleaning supplies should be kept away from heat sources like stovetops and space heaters.
3. Keep your electrical wiring in good condition
Faulty wiring is a common cause of house fires. Have a professional electrician inspect your home's wiring every few years to make sure that it is in good condition.
4. Don't leave cooking unattended
Cooking is a leading cause of house fires. Never leave food cooking on the stove unattended, and always turn off the stove before leaving the kitchen.
5. Create a fire escape plan
In case of a fire, every member of your household should know how to escape safely. Create a fire escape plan and practice it regularly.
